VARIATION C: WAVE SPLASH (Cascading Motion)
================================================================
Duration: 1.5 seconds (45 frames total)

Key differences from standard:
- Particle count: 100-140 particles (layered bursts)
- Velocity: 300-350 px/s initial, then -70% decay
- Pattern: Layered horizontal spray, 3-4 distinct wave layers
- Delay between waves: 200ms (6 frames)
- Burst pattern: Horizontal layered release vs omnidirectional
- Frame count: 45 total (extended)
- Ripple pattern: Multiple concentric ripples (one per wave)
- Use case: Water flowing over obstacles, wave motion, cascading effect

Frame breakdown (Wave):
Frames 1-3: Anticipation (90ms)
Frames 4-9: Wave 1 burst (180ms)
Frames 10-15: Wave 2 burst (180ms, overlapping)
Frames 16-21: Wave 3 burst (180ms, overlapping)
Frames 22-30: Cascade settling (240ms)
Frames 31-45: Ripple dissipation (450ms)

Wave pattern:
- Wave 1: Full omnidirectional burst (180 degrees horizontal)
- Wave 2: Layered above wave 1 (80% of particle count)
- Wave 3: Layered above wave 2 (60% of particle count)
- Each wave: Offset 200ms apart, creates cascading effect

AUDIO SYNCHRONIZATION REQUIREMENTS
================================================================================

CRITICAL: Frame-perfect sync with impact sound

Sound effect requirements:
- Format: WAV (lossless) or high-quality MP3
- Bitrate: 16-bit 44.1kHz minimum
- Duration: 0.5-0.8 seconds (main impact phase)
- Frequency characteristics:
  - Low bass: 40-120 Hz (water body impact)
  - Mid-range: 200-500 Hz (splash sound)
  - High-range: 2000-5000 Hz (water spray/droplets)

Sync points:
- T+0ms: Impact sound begins (Frame 1)
- T+100ms: Burst peak (Frame 4, match sound intensity)
- T+300ms: Particles at maximum spread (Frame 10, sound fading)
- T+500ms: Particle settling (Frame 16, sound mostly faded)
- T+800ms: Ripple effect (Frame 25, barely audible water sound)

Audio levels:
- T+0-300ms: Full volume (-3dB to 0dB)
- T+300-500ms: 60% volume fade (-10dB to -15dB)
- T+500-800ms: 20% volume minimum (-25dB to -30dB)
- T+800ms+: Fade to silence (-40dB)
