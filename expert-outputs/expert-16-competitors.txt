## The One Counterintuitive Finding

> **Lower volume + higher craft = exponentially more revenue per video.**
>
> MagnatesMedia posts 2–3×/month and earns ~$25–75K/month.
> A daily AITJ TTS channel at 150+ videos/month earns ~$3–9K/month.
>
> **That's a 10× revenue difference for 50× less content.** The quality multiplier is real — and it compounds through SEO longevity (a MagnatesMedia video earns views for 3–5 years; a TTS Reddit clip earns views for 3–5 days).
